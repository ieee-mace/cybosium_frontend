Competitions

Bot Brew

Get to design and build a mini robot driven by your imagination. This competition is all about creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork. Participants will have a fixed time frame to design and build their project using the provided kit. The teams will also be assigned a mentor who will guide them through the process and help them troubleshoot any issues they encounter during the build.

Robopitch

Pitch your ideas and express your creativity to the fullest! Robopitch is an intensive brainstorming event where individuals from different backgrounds, skills, and interests converge to diagnose predefined problems, identify the best opportunities, and ideate the most viable solution for topics related to the tracks of TEP. So dust off your robotics skills, and get ready to turn junk into a masterpiece.

Student Project Display

Get ready to showcase your brilliance at Cybosium as the ultimate Student Project display competition is here. It's time to unleash your creativity, innovation and skills, and present your best projects in front of a panel of experts. With exciting prizes up for grabs, this is the perfect opportunity to shine and make a mark in the academic world. So gear up, put on your thinking caps, and join us at Cybosium - where brilliance meets recognition!

Video Contest

Participants can choose any one of the following topics Evolution of drones in 60 sec, Wearable robotics: the future of cyborg era

Video Duration should not exceed 1 minute and should be in portrait mode.Winners will be choosen based on the content of the video and the likes it received

Video will be posted on IGTV section of cybosium insta handle. Submission deadline March 10 2023

Robo Rumble

Get set for an exciting debate competition that promises to be both engaging and thought-provoking. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their argumentative and critical thinking skills in front of a panel of judges and a live audience. If you can effectively communicate your position, manifest your mastery of the topic, and command good presentation skills, then the stage is all yours.

Cybosium, India's first-ever IEEE RAS Technical Education Program, will be a culmination of technical and non-technical events organized in collaboration with the leading companies and organizations in the robotics and automation industry
